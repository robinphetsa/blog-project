About Me

Hi there! My name is Robin. I'm a statistician turned programmer whose end goal is to practice data science. Despite my techincal background, I also have a major passion for writing (hence me starting this blog). I went through multiple bouts of depression throughout my young life, at least three major episodes through the span of 6 and a half years. Despite the bad times, there were moments where I was able to keep depression at bay.

After living with it for so long, I had slowly began to realize what worked for me and what didn't. I'm going to try my absolute best to convey the things I learned in my journey coping with clinical depression: how to cope, who or where to turn to, addressing the root of the issue(s). Please remember that the advice and opinions on this blog are my own. I am NOT a professional mental health expert or a doctor of anykind, just a normal guy trying to find and help others find their happy place. If you would like to reach out to me personally, please feel free to email me at robinphetsa@gmail.com
